Breaded Pork Tenderloin

Description

This dish uses the most tender cut of pork, breaded and fried in a pan with butter

The breading made with egg and crushed up soda crackers crisps up really nicely when fried. This creates a contrast between the crispy outside, and incredibly tender pork inside. It all comes together to make an unforgettable dish.

Ingredients

  • Strip of pork tenderloin
  • 5-6 Whisked whole eggs
  • 1-2 bags of soda crackers
  • 3Tbsp of butter (to fry in)

Steps

  1. Slice your pork tenderloin strip into even single portion sized pieces.
  2. Use meat mallet to tenderize your pieces of tenderloin
  3. Crush up your soda crackers so that they are in pieces roughly .5 - 1 cm in diameter.
  4. Whisk your whole eggs in a bowl
  5. Heat pan and butter over medium-high heat
  6. Dip strips of tenderloin in your egg, and roll them in the crushed crackers, so as to form a breading
  7. Place breaded pieces in the pan, and cook until one side is golden brown, flip and repeat on other side
  8. Remove fully cooked pieces from pan, let sit for 2-3 minutes, and enjoy!
