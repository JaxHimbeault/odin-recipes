Grilled Cheese

Description

Simple, yet delicious. A staple in kitchens all around the world, this combination of simple ingredients comes together to form something truly amazing

Made by frying two buttered pieces of bread with plenty of cheese between them. The type of bread and cheese used is a topic of great debate, but ultimately, it comes down to personal preference!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (thick sliced)
  • Butter/Margarine (enough to coat outsides of bread slices)
  • Choice of cheese (hard cheeses work best when melted)

Steps

  1. Heat a medium sized pan on the stove (medium heat).
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Slice your cheese of choice to desired thickness.
  4. Place one slice of bread in heated pan, buttered side down.
  5. Place cheese evenly on top of slice of bread in the pan, and place your other slice of bread on top. (the slices of bread should make a sandwich, with the buttered sides out. This will help them brown up nicely).
  6. Wait for the cheese to melt just enough to keep the bread slices together, and flip to cook the other side
  7. Continue to watch and flip, until both sides of the sandwich are golden brown.
  8. Remove from pan, slice in half, and enjoy!
