Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

Description

This classic dish includes a meat and tomato sauce, served over al dente spaghetti

The sauce can be made with your choice of beef, chicken or pork, and is seasoned with garlic, onions and fresh basil.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of fresh ground beef (can substitute chicken or pork)
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 6 ounces tomato paste
  • whole chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup fresh chopped basil
  • 1 cup water
  • box of dried spaghetti
  • 1/2 clove sliced garlic

Steps

  1. Heat large saucepan over medium-high heat and sautee your chopped garlic in a small amount of olive oil
  2. place your meat in the pan and stir until all of the meat is browned
  3. add tomato sauce, tomato paste, water and sugar into the pan, and stir
  4. add remaining ingredients to pan, simmer covered for 30 mins - 1 hour, stirring occasionally
  5. Boil pasta in seperate pot, cook until tender, but still firm (al dente)
  6. strain pasta, and add a small amount of pasta water to the simmering sauce (to thicken). Stir
  7. plate the pasta, remove the sauce from heat, and scoop over the spaghetti
  8. season to preference with freshly ground black pepper, and serve!
